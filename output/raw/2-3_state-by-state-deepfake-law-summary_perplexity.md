Across all states, deepfake laws cluster into three main buckets:

Non‑consensual sexual/synthetic intimate images (including deepfake pornography and AI‑generated child sexual abuse material).

Political and election‑related synthetic media, especially deceptive AI‑generated audio/video of candidates.

Personality/likeness and publicity‑rights style protections for digital replicas.

How to use the risk buckets

For market entry, a practical framework is:

Strict / “avoid or be very careful”

Multiple deepfake‑specific statutes (sexual + election + likeness), often with felony‑level penalties or long sentences and broad civil liability.
